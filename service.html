Compensation and Damages

In law, "Compensation and Damages" refers to a legal claim where a party
seeks monetary payment to be reimbursed for losses or injuries suffered due
to another party's wrongful actions, usually arising from a Breach of
Contract or a tort, with "Compensation" aiming to restore the injured party to
their original position, while "damages" specifically represent the monetary
award given for the harm suffered; essentially, compensation can
encompass a claim for damages as one element of seeking redress.

We have Expertise in Compensation and Damages Claim in Variety of
Sectors including Motor Accident Claim Case, Railway Accident Claim Case
etc.

Banking and SARFAESI Matter

The Securitisation and Reconstruction of Financial Assets and
Enforcement of Security Interest Act (SARFAESI Act) of 2002 provides
a framework for Banks and Financial Institutions to recover loans
from borrowers who default. The Act also gives borrowers the right to
appeal if they feel their rights have been violated.

Matter relating to any Banking Issue including Recovery of Financial
Debts of various Banking and Non Banking Financial Institutions
before the DRT, DRAT and competent Court of Law.
